The word <b>можна</b> has no exact translation in English, but it means something like [Is it possible to?] and is used to request permission.

<p>

<b>можна позичити твою машину?</b> would translate word-for-word to [Is-it-possible-to borrow your car?] 

<p>

<b>Поїдеш</b> is a perfective verb meaning [will go (by vehicle)].  The Ukrainian language makes a distinction between going by foot and going by vehicle).
