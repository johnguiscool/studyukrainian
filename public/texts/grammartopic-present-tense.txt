<h3>Present Tense</h3>
<p>The present tense in Ukrainian is equivalent to both the present tense and the present progressive tense in English. <strong>я працюю </strong>can mean [I work] or [I am working].</p>
<p>Verbs can be conjugated in the present tense according to the tables below.</p>

<p><strong>Examples:</strong></p>
<p> - <strong>Що ти робиш?</strong><br /> - <em>What are you doing?</em>
<br />
- <strong> Я читаю книгу.</strong><br /> - <em>I am reading a book.</em></p>

<p> - <strong>Ти студент?</strong><br /> - <em>Are you a student?</em>
<br />
 - <strong>Ні, я працюю.</strong><br /> - <em>No, I work.</em></p>
