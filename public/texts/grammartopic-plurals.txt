<h3>Plurals</h3>
<p>In general, plurals are formed by adding either <strong>-</strong><strong>и</strong> or <strong>-і</strong> to the end of a noun.</p>
<table class="table">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>фільм</strong><strong>&rarr; фільми</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p>film &rarr; films</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>книга</strong><strong>&rarr; книги</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p>book &rarr; books</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>кот &rarr; коти</strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p>cat &rarr; cats</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
<p>Some final consonants may change when the <strong>-</strong><strong>и</strong> / <strong>-і </strong>suffix is added:</p>
<table class="table">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>
<p><strong>друг</strong><strong>&rarr; друзі </strong></p>
</td>
<td>
<p>friend &rarr; friends</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
