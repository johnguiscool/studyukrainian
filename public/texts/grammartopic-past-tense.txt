<h3>Past Tense</h3>
<p>The past tense is very simple to form in Ukrainian. You simply remove the suffix of the verb and replace it with an ending corresponding to the gender of the subject.</p>

<h3>Examples:</h3>
- <strong>Що ти робив на вихідних? </strong>(spoken to a man)<br>
- <em>What did you do over the weekend?</em><p>
- <strong>Я їздив в Карпати!</strong> (spoken by a man)<br>
- <em>I went to the Carpathians!</em><p>
- <strong>Що ти робила на вихідних?</strong> (spoken to a woman)<br>
- <em>What did you do over the weekend?</em><p>
- <strong>Я читала книгу.</strong> (spoken by a woman)<br>
- <em>I read a book.</em>
