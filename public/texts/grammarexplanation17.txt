There are two ways to say that you like something.  You've already learned one of them.  

<p>

<b></b> <br>  
<b>Ти любиш Львів?</b>  
<p>

We can also use the word <b>подобається</b> which means [pleases] or [is pleasing to].

<p>

<b>Тобі подобається Львів</b> means [You like Lviv] or, more literally, [Lviv is pleasing to you].

<br>

<b>Мені подобається Львів</b> means [I like Lviv] or, more literally, [Lviv is pleasing to me].  

<p>

If no object is specified, as in the question:
<br>
<b>Мені подобається?</b>
<br>
The phrase just means [It is pleasing to me].
