The question word <b>Який</b> and <b>Яка</b> can mean [Which?] in addition to [How?].  <p>

<b>Який твій улюблений фільм?</b> translates word-for-word to [Which your favorite movie?], in the sense of [What is your favorite movie?]

<br>

<b>Яка твоя улюблена книжка?</b> translates word-for-word to [Which your favorite book?], in the sense of [What is your favorite book?]
