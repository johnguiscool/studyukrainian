There are two ways to express the future tense in Ukrainian.  One is to use the perfective form of a verb.  The second is to use the verb <b>буду ___</b>, which means [will be doing ___].

<p>

<b>я буду ___</b> means [I will be doing ___].

<br>

<b>ти будеш ___</b> means [You will be doing ___].

<p>

<b>я буду читати книгу</b> means [I will be reading a book].
<br>
<b>Що ти будеш робити завтра?</b> means [What will you be doing tomorrow?].
